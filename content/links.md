### Amateur Radio
I am a Technician Class Amateur Radio Operator. I get my Radio news and events related to [ARRL](https://www.arrl.org/home/).

For those who do not know, [ARRL](https://www.arrl.org/home/) is a non-commercial organization of radio amateurs. You can read more [about ARRL](https://www.arrl.org/about-arrl/).

Here is a link to the [Band Plan](https://www.arrl.org/band-plan/) page, which tells you about the voluntary division of a band to avoid interference between incompatible modes.
